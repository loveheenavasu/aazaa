Practical, effective therapy, coaching
& couples therapy in English and Dutch.

Life for an expat in Holland isn´t always easy and quite often pretty complex, therefore it isn´t that unusual that many expats don´t manage so well just by themselves at some point.
As an expatriate you have to deal not only with all the normal problems in life, but with the difficulties that come with being a stranger in a strange country aswell.
Understanding cultural differences and identifying your own blind spots and habitual patterns is very difficult, which often results in a lack of overview and an inability to see solutions clearly.
The Centrumpraktijk helps you to increase your insight in your thoughts, feelings and behavior and to obtain clarity in difficult (Dutch) situations and complicated relationships.
Using practical and effective tools you will be helped to address and tackle the difficulties and challenges you are facing both as an expat and an individual in your work and personal life.
This allows you to find a new balance and to (re)gain your strength and direction, making you feel more energetic, stronger and self-confident, which will benefit you now and in the future.
