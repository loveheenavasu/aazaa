Practical, effective therapy, coaching & couples therapy

in English and Dutch.


Life for an expat in Holland isn´t always easy and quite often pretty complex,
therefore it isn´t that unusual that many expats don´t manage so well at some point.

As an expatriate you have to deal not only with all the normal problems in life, but
with the difficulties that come with being a stranger in a strange country as well.

It can be difficult to understand cultural differences and to identify your own blind
spots as well, which makes it hard to keep an overview & tackle the issues at hand.



The Centrumpraktijk helps you to increase your insight in your thoughts, feelings
and behavior and to obtain clarity in difficult (Dutch) situations and relationships.

Using practical and effective tools you will be helped to address and tackle the
difficulties and challenges you are facing in both your work and personal life.

This allows you to find a new balance and to (re)gain your strength, enjoyment
and direction, making you feel more energetic, stronger and self-confident.
