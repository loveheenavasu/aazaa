Almost everyone has a dysfunctional pattern and it can make a big difference when it learns to recognize. You will then understand better why you every time the same problems and feelings come into your life, work and relationships - unconsciously repeat once simply a learned pattern and the consequences can be very negative. Then you will be helped in your daily life, this pattern will change, so you no longer undermines hampered and restricted.

It is very important to learn to deal with your 'dysfunctional' patterns. That is to cope with the habits in the way you think, feel and do you undermine, impede and / or limit - and to recognize, understand and tackle. At the Centre Practice you will be helped to the patterns that you undermine and obstruct the boss. This is a very practical and very effective method that allows you with some practice and dedication soon your problems to tackle. And which furthermore makes you independent of any therapy or Course because you helped your own methodology to make it so you can go yourself.

~

There are various dysfunctional patterns and known examples include perfectionism and anxiety. A dysfunctional pattern as anxiety can make someone very frightened of new things to do or to make mistakes, which he or she comes nowhere and far below the actual level of functioning. Perfectionism example can make someone puts the bar too high and the feeling never good enough or not good enough to do. Et cetera.

~

You will be helped to change your sabotaging ideas and habits, to accept difficult facts and to strengthen your weaknesses. You will get tools that allow to tackle difficult situations in an insightful and practical way. You learn better to cope with difficult feelings and to to process and direct your emotions. This greatly eliminates problems such as stress, fear, anxiety, fatigue and exhaustion and makes you calmer, stronger, more energetic and more confident in your life, work and relationships.
